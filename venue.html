Location:

The Meeting will take place from April 10 to 12, 2015, and will be held in Hotel H10 Taburiente Playa, in La Palma, Canary Islands, Spain.

Accommodation:

There are two possibilities for accommodation.

We ask all participants to make there own bookings through the hotel. We recommend that you book half pension – the price is very similar to breakfast only, and we expect that most of us will stay in those places and eat together. How to get to La Palma:

There are multiple ways to get to La Palma by plane:

  1. Take a direct flight from Europe. Make sure you fly to Santa Cruz de La Palma (airport code SPC), and not to Palma de Mallorca, Las Palmas de Gran Canaria, or some other place with palm trees.
  2. Fly to Tenerife Norte (TFN) and take a connecting flight to La Palma.
  3. Fly to Tenerife Sur (TFS), take a bus or a taxi to Tenerife Norte Airport (TFN), there are frequent flights to La Palma from there.

Some more information (partly dated) can be found here: Travel Information, Travel to La Palma

 

Transport from the airport to the Hotel Taburiente Playa:

There are two options to get from the airport in La Palma to the hotel:

1. Taxi: Available outside arrivals. Cost less than €10.

2. Bus: From arrivals, take line number 500 in the direction of Santa Cruz de La Palma. Ask the driver to let you know when you are at the Hotel Taburiente, Los Cancajos. Journey takes about 8 mins.
